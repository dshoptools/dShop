About dShop

Welcome to dShop, your friendly neighborhood hub for simple and effective file utilities. Our mission is to make document management accessible to everyone, everywhere. We believe that you shouldn't need complicated software or expensive subscriptions to perform essential tasks like compressing a PDF or converting an image. That's why we created a suite of fast, free, and secure browser-based tools. We are dedicated to providing a seamless experience with a focus on privacy and ease of use.
